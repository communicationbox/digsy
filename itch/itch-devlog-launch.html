It's out. Digsy World is now playable in your browser — no download, no sign-up, no rush.

You're Digsy, a small archaeologist with a shovel and a lot of curiosity. Dig anywhere across an endless, procedurally-generated world — golden meadows, bone deserts, ashen woods, red lands, ancient marshes and frozen wastes — unearth raw fossils, and carry them to the Museum to be identified. Every species you complete lights up its own rotating 3D voxel skeleton in your Fossil Book.

Then the fun part: mix skulls, bodies and legs into chimeras with names of their own, gather a species' DNA to awaken it, and watch your creations wander the town park — some even swim in the pond.

Try these first

  • Dig in three different biomes and see how the finds change.
  • Take your fossils to a town with a Museum and complete your first skeleton.
  • Buy a treasure map and follow the X to a guaranteed rare find.
  • Assemble your first chimera and give it a look you like at the barber and tailor.

What it is (and isn't)

Cozy and low-pressure by design: no combat, no game over. Six biomes, 60+ species, day/night and seasons, boats, a bike and a rideable flying fossil, dark caves with their own creatures. It plays in the browser, installs as an app, and works offline — the world is procedural and your save lives on your device.

Controls

Move: WASD / arrow keys / on-screen joystick  ·  Interact & dig: E or the on-screen A button  ·  Fossil Book: L  ·  Bag: I
