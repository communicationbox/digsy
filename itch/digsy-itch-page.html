Digsy World

A cozy pixel-art game about digging up the past.

No combat, no losing, no rush — just wander, dig, and discover.

🪏Dig up a forgotten world

You're Digsy, a small archaeologist exploring an endless, procedurally-generated world — golden meadows, bone deserts, ashen woods, red lands, ancient marshes and frozen wastes, each with its own creatures. Dig anywhere, unearth raw fossils, and carry them to the Museum to be identified. Every species you complete lights up its rotating 3D voxel skeleton in your Fossil Book.

🦴Build creatures that never existed

Mix and match skulls, bodies and legs to assemble chimeras with names of their own. Gather a species' DNA to awaken it, and watch your creations roam the town park — some even swim in its pond.

🏛️Cozy towns, boats and a flying fossil

Wander town squares with a central fountain, shops, a museum and a barber. Ride a boat, a bike, or a flying fossil mount, and chase legendary finds hidden under treasure-map X's and deep in dark caves. The world map colours every biome so you always know where to head next.

🎨Make Digsy yours

Pick a look — hats, shirts, hairstyles and colours — free at the start, with themed cosmetics to discover across the biomes.

✨Features

  • Infinite, hand-tuned procedural world — 6 biomes, 60+ species
  • 3D voxel skeletons you complete piece by piece at the Museum
  • Chimeras & awakening — bring extinct creatures back to life
  • Treasure maps, caves, day/night cycle and seasons
  • Boats, a bike, and a rideable flying fossil
  • Cozy and low-pressure by design — no combat, no game over
  • Plays in your browser · installable · works offline (PWA)

Controls — Move: WASD / arrows / on-screen joystick · Interact & dig: E or the on-screen A button · Fossil Book: L · Bag: I

Languages — English · Italiano · Русский
